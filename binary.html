Instructions

To use this tool:

1. Enter the text or binary you want to convert in the input field above.

2. Click "Convert to Binary" to convert text to binary.

3. Click "Convert to Text" to convert binary to text.

4. The result will be displayed below.

5. Click "Reset" to clear the input and hide the result.
