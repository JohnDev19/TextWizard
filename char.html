How to Use

1. Enter or paste your text in the text area above.

2. Click on one of the conversion buttons to transform your text:

  • Uppercase: Converts all characters to uppercase.
  • Lowercase: Converts all characters to lowercase.
  • Proper Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word.
  • Reverse: Reverses the order of characters in the text.

3. Use the copy button (ðŸ“‹) to copy the converted text to your clipboard.

4. Click 'Reset' to clear the text area and start over.
